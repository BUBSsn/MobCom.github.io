CIST Student Data Privacy Policy

At the Central Institute of Science and Technology (CIST), we take your privacy seriously. When you register as a student, you trust us with your personal information. This policy explains how we use and protect that data.

1. Introduction

The Central Institute of Science and Technology (CIST) respects and protects the privacy rights of its students in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data when you apply for admission, register, and study at CIST.

2. Information We Collect

When you register or enrol at CIST, we may collect and process the following types of data:
- Personal Information: full name, date of birth, gender, nationality, address, and contact details.
- Student Records: academic history, grades, class schedules, and attendance.
- Identification Documents: government-issued IDs, school IDs, photos.
- Financial Information: tuition payments, scholarships, and financial aid records.
- Health and Emergency Data: medical records and emergency contacts (if necessary).
- Digital & Security Data: login credentials for learning systems, CCTV footage, and campus security records.

3. Purpose of Processing Your Data

We process your personal information to:
- Evaluate and process student applications and enrolments.
- Manage academic records, class registration, and student progress.
- Provide student services such as libraries, laboratories, and online learning platforms.
- Support guidance, counseling, and health services.
- Process payments, scholarships, and other financial transactions.
- Ensure campus safety and security.
- Comply with reporting requirements of the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), Department of Education (DepEd), and other government agencies.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

CIST will not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share your information with:
- Government Agencies: CHED, DepEd, Bureau of Immigration, or other lawful authorities.
- Accreditation & Professional Bodies: for program recognition and compliance.
- Service Providers: IT platforms, cloud storage, and communication tools (under strict confidentiality agreements).
- Emergency Services: hospitals, clinics, or law enforcement when necessary.

5. Data Retention

Your personal data will be retained only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes stated above, or as required by law. Once no longer needed, data will be securely deleted, anonymized, or archived in accordance with CIST policies.

6. Data Protection Measures

CIST employs appropriate organizational, physical, and technical measures to ensure your data is protected, including:
- Encrypted digital records and secure servers
- Controlled access to student files
- Regular system monitoring and security audits
- Staff training on privacy and data protection

7. Your Rights Under the Data Privacy Act

As a student, you have the right to:
- Be informed about how your data is collected and used.
- Access your personal information upon request.
- Correct or Rectify inaccurate or outdated information.
- Request Erasure of personal data, where applicable.
- Object to the processing of your personal information.
- File a Complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) if you believe your rights have been violated.

8. Policy Updates

CIST may revise this Student Privacy Policy to comply with new regulations or improve our practices. Updates will be posted on the official CIST website and student portals.
